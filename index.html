Portfolio

These are just some of my favourite projects, for more, you can checkout my github page or email me, I'll be happy to show you more of my work.

"Uma App para o Joãozinho"

Cross-Platform Mobile game developed for the course of "Laboratório de Gestão de Projectos" at FEUP. A project suggested by the non-profit organization "Um Lugar para o Joãozinho" with the purpose of gathering funds in order to rebuild, requalify and improve the pediatric wing of Hospital S. Joao in Porto. The project was developed using the Unity framework, and was meant to use the concept of gamification to incite users to donate to the organization. My team's colaboration in this project was discontinued after the course was done and continued by the organization.
Learn more in "Um Lugar para o Joãozinho" website.

Marketing Plan for "Up2Fit" Gym

This project is not related with technology at all, it was developed for the course "Marketing" at FEUP and it's objective was to make a Marketing Strategy Analysis of a real business. I decided to showcase this project since it's very different from my other projects and it taught me a lot about the importance of marketing in a real life situation. This study included devicing a marketing plan, analysing the internal and external enviornment of the small gym, analysing the customers and market segment and, finally, defining a marketing strategy accordingly. For this study we used real data provided by the owner and the final strategic decisions were really implemented to (hopefully) help the business grow.
Find out more about the gym at their facebook page.

Up2Manage

Information system using the PRIMAVERA ERP. This project was developed for the course of "Sistemas de Informação" at FEUP and the goal was to understand the complexity of organizational Enterprise systems. The theme was Sales Force Automation so we developed an interactive dashboard webapp to help the Sales Team design sales routes, accept tasks layed out by their Supervisors, input sales data, create reservations and complete Orders. This app would work with the enterprise server dealing with product stock and any data irrelevant for the Sales Team making their jobs easier and more efficient.

Places4All

Website to evaluate places and locations according to their acessibility to users with limited mobility. This project was developed for the "Coding for Social Impact" project in the "Laboratório de Desenvolvimento de Software" course at FEUP. This website would combine official audits with user reviews and ratings for different places, keeping the evaluation up to date and providing real-time live information on obstacles on route to access the location. This website was developed using ASP.NET since it was mostly for display purposes, and we developed a small Android app to be used on the official audits, where a certified user could answer a comlpete survey and upload pictures to prove it's ratings.
Learn more in "Places4all" website.
